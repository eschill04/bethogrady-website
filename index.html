ABOUT

I am primarily a painter and sculptor. I enjoy working with shapes, patterns, and colors. Most of my artwork is nonrepresentational or abstract. I strive to create a strong visual impact using simple structures. During the past four years I experimented with corrugated cardboard, and cutting wood with a scroll saw. I also made stop motion videos with some of the cardboard and wood pieces, where I explored movement, sound, and story telling. In 2025, I am drawing with graphite and colored pencil, and painting with acrylic and charcoal on canvas and wood panel.

STUDIO

My studio is located in the attic of my New Jersey home. It's a peaceful place that overlooks trees, farmland, and distant hills.

BIO

Beth O'Grady was born in 1960, raised in Princeton, and lives with her husband and two children in Tewksbury, New Jersey. She earned a BFA from Parsons School of Design/The New School, NYC, and a BS from The Pennsylvania State University. O'Grady formerly worked as an editorial illustrator for magazines and newspapers, and as a graphic designer. She shows her artwork locally and internationally.
